Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and combine with pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Serve with extra cheese and freshly ground black pepper.
Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Chicken Caesar Salad

A healthy and delicious salad with grilled chicken, crisp lettuce, and creamy Caesar dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 100g romaine lettuce
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Croutons

Instructions:

  1. Grill the chicken breasts until fully cooked and slice them.
  2. Toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing in a bowl.
  3. Add sliced chicken and top with croutons and Parmesan.
Preparation Time: 15 minutes
