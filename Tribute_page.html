Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"Missile Man of India" and 11th President of India

Biography

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs, earning him the nickname "Missile Man of India."
